Plot: when Elle falls in love with Noah, her best friend's brother, risks to lose Lee's friendship or Noah's love...

Duration: about 105 minutes (the first movie)
about 131 minutes (the second movie)

My opinion: Honestly, I don't know why I watched the second movie. The first one is awful.
Every scene is so full of clichès, misogyny and stereotypes, it's just too much. The plot is a complete disaster, not evena plot twist, it follows the mundane, stereotypical, boring high school love story. And I didn't need another one.
Moreover, in every moment you can guess what is going to happen and, when it happens, you can do nothing but roll your eyes.
Maybe the beginning wasn't that bad, but then...
And that was about the first movie.
Actually, I don't know why I watched the sequel. Wait — I do: the first was so bad it made me laugh. I laughed a lot.
Anyway, the beginning of the second movie was interesting, it suggested something new and unexpected. Sadly, everything was expected. It repeated all the mediocre things of the first one, as if the watcher would have enjoyed them if seen another time. Well, I didn't enjoy them. The end was disappointing and bad as the rest of the movie.
It made me laugh.
After ruining the second movie they'll make a third one. Wonderful. I guess I'll watch it only to know if it can save the series, but I dubt it.
